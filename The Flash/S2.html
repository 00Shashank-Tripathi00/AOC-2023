The Flash - Season 2

The series follows Allen, portrayed by Grant Gustin, The second season of The Flash we see Barry recognized as a hero in Central City after saving the city, only to face a new threat from a parallel universe in the form of the speedster Zoom played by Teddy Sears, who seeks to eliminate everyone connected to the Speed Force throughout the multiverse.

Joined by main cast members Candice Patton, Danielle Panabaker, Carlos Valdes, Tom Cavanagh, Jesse L. Martin, Keiynan Lonsdale and Violett Bean.
