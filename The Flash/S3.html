The Flash - Season 3

The third season of the American television series The Flash, which is based on Barry Allen/Flash portrayed by Grant Gustin,
a superhero crime-fighter with the power to move at super speeds, follows Barry as he deals with the consequences of his decision to save his mother, which creates a timeline called "Flashpoint", and the resulting emergence of a new foe in Savitar.

Joined by main cast members Candice Patton, Danielle Panabaker, Carlos Valdes, Tom Cavanagh, Jesse L. Martin, Keiynan Lonsdale, Violett Bean and Tom Felton.
